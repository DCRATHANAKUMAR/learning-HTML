Manual

To turn the pages on your PC, drag the corners of the document. On mobile devices, swipe from left to right or right to left. Alternatively you can use the arrow buttons. To go to a specific page, click on a page in the table of contents or enter a page number in the page number indicator at the top of the screen.
Click here to open a list of all documents.
Click on this icon to open a specific document by entering its product, edition and pub date. The desired document opens automatically once a selection has been made for these three filters.
Click on this icon to open the table of contents for the current document.
Click on this icon to search for content in the current document. Enter a search term consisting of at least three characters in the text field to start the full text search.
Click on this icon to open a dialog for downloading individual pages of the document as a PDF.
Use this option to show all the clickable areas within the document.
To zoom in and out of a page on your mobile device, pinch and stretch two fingers over the page area, or turn your mouse wheel on your PC. To move the visible page section, press and drag it with your mouse (on PCs) or your finger (on mobile devices). Double-click outside the clickable area on a page to zoom in to 100% or out to its original size again. If you zoom in or out on a page, you cannot swipe to turn the pages on mobile devices.
Click on this icon to log off the CX epaper and return to the login page.
